# Linkedin Feed Block
"Display content of a Linkedin feed."

![Linkedin Feed Block](Screenshots/Linkedin%20Feed%20Block%20-%20icon.png)

A *Block Type* designed to display content of a Linkedin feed.

--------------|------|---------------
